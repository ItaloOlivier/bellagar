About

Rising Star of
British Triathlon

Isabella "Bella" Gar is an elite junior triathlete representing Great Britain on the international stage.

A dual British-Italian national, Bella races domestically in both the UK and Italy. Currently based in Italy, she trains under coach Massimo Strada as part of Team 707 Minini. Her dedication to swimming, cycling, and running continues to push her toward new heights in international triathlon competition.
